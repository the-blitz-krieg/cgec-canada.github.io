Why the UK for Canadian Business

The United Kingdom offers Canadian businesses perhaps the most natural international expansion destination: shared legal frameworks, common language, Commonwealth relationships, and a deep-rooted business culture that values Canadian partnerships.

Post-Brexit, the UK is actively building bilateral trade relationships and Canadian businesses are well-positioned to benefit from this openness.

  • Shared common law legal system
  • Commonwealth trade relationship frameworks
  • English-language market — no translation barrier
  • UK-Canada trade actively expanding post-Brexit
  • EICBI parliamentary relationships in Westminster

Why the EEA for Canadian Business

The Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement (CETA) between Canada and the European Union provides Canadian businesses with preferential access to the world's largest single market — removing tariffs on 98% of product categories.

CGEC's exclusive partnership with EICBI, an EU Transparency Register-accredited organisation, provides institutional access that goes far beyond what CETA alone can offer.

  • CETA provides 98% tariff-free access to EU markets
  • EICBI's European Parliament accreditation
  • Direct access to EU institutional decision-makers
  • Regulatory harmonisation support across member states
  • 27+ country market through a single entry strategy

Why India for Canadian Business

India is the world's most populous nation and one of its fastest-growing major economies. For Canadian businesses, India represents a generational opportunity — particularly given Canada's large Indian diaspora and deep bilateral ties.

What makes CGEC's India offering unique is EICBI's 13-year, ground-level relationship network with Indian government bodies, business chambers, and institutional leaders across key Indian cities.

  • World's fastest-growing major economy
  • Strong Canada-India diaspora and cultural connections
  • EICBI's 13-year institutional network across India
  • Relationships with Indian government and business bodies
  • Engagement with regional governments across Indian states

Trade Agreements

The CETA Advantage for Canadian Exporters

The Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement (CETA) between Canada and the European Union is one of the most significant trade agreements Canada has ever signed — yet most Canadian SMEs are not fully leveraging it.

CGEC helps Canadian businesses understand and maximise their CETA entitlements, from tariff elimination to professional recognition, procurement access, and regulatory cooperation.

What CETA Delivers for Canadian Business

  • 98% of tariff lines eliminated immediately
  • Access to EU government procurement markets
  • Mutual recognition of professional qualifications
  • Intellectual property protections strengthened
  • Investment protection mechanisms
  • Regulatory cooperation to reduce non-tariff barriers
  • Temporary entry for business persons
