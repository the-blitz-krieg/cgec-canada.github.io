Our Mandate

Connecting Canadian Enterprise to the World

The Canada Global Export Council (CGEC) is a not-for-profit organisation established to guide Canadian businesses — from emerging SMEs to established enterprises — through the complexities of international market entry.

Through our exclusive Canadian partnership with the Europe India Centre for Business & Industry (EICBI), CGEC provides unparalleled access to institutional relationships across the United Kingdom, European Union, and India that would otherwise take decades to build.

Read our mandate

Market Intelligence

Regulatory landscapes, tariff structures, market entry requirements, and competitive intelligence across UK, EEA, and Indian markets.

Institutional Access

Direct connections to Members of the European Parliament, British parliamentarians, diplomats, and senior business leaders through EICBI's convening power.

Trade Mission Support

Structured trade missions to Brussels, London, and key Indian cities, with curated B2B matchmaking and government introductions.

Advisory & Compliance

Expert guidance on legal structures, compliance frameworks, and business registration across target jurisdictions.

Strategic Partnership

Backed by Europe's Most Connected India-EU Forum

CGEC is the exclusive Canadian partner of the Europe India Centre for Business & Industry (EICBI) — a European Commission registered geopolitical forum with 13 years of convening high-level parliamentary and business dialogue across the Europe-India corridor.

Through EICBI, CGEC members gain access to summits at the European Parliament in Brussels and the British Parliament in London, direct engagement with Members of the European Parliament, and a network spanning thousands of business and government leaders.
