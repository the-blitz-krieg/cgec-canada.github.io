Our Mandate

Connecting Canadian Enterprise to the World

The Canada Global Export Council (CGEC) is a not-for-profit organisation established to guide Canadian businesses through the complexities of international market entry into the UK, EEA, and India.

Through our exclusive Canadian partnership with EICBI, we provide unparalleled access to institutional relationships that would otherwise take decades to build.

Strategic Partnership

Backed by Europe's Most Connected India-EU Forum

CGEC is the exclusive Canadian partner of EICBI — a European Commission registered geopolitical forum with 13 years of parliamentary-level dialogue across the Europe-India corridor.

Through EICBI, CGEC members gain access to summits at the European Parliament and British Parliament, direct engagement with MEPs, and a network spanning thousands of business and government leaders.
