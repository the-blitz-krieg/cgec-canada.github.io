About EICBI

Europe's Most Credentialled India-EU Trade Forum

The Europe India Centre for Business & Industry (EICBI) is a European Commission registered geopolitical forum dedicated to advancing Track 1.5 Diplomacy — the critical space between official government channels and private sector engagement.

Founded over 13 years ago, EICBI has built an unmatched institutional presence at the intersection of European and Indian trade, investment, and policy. It operates across the India-UK and India-EU corridors, convening the leaders who shape these relationships.

For CGEC members, this translates directly: your Canadian business enters these corridors backed by institutional credibility that took 13 years to build.
