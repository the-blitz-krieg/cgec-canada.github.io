Our Mission

A Council Built on Institutional Credibility

The Canada Global Export Council (CGEC) was established to address a critical gap: Canadian businesses — particularly SMEs — possess world-class products and services, but lack the institutional relationships, regulatory knowledge, and on-the-ground networks required to succeed in complex international markets.

CGEC exists to close that gap. As the exclusive Canadian partner of the Europe India Centre for Business & Industry (EICBI), CGEC provides 13 years of parliamentary-level relationships, EU institutional access, and proven trade corridor expertise directly to Canadian exporters.

We are a not-for-profit organisation. Our mandate is the success of Canadian business on the global stage — not shareholder returns.

"Canada's businesses are globally competitive. What they need is a credible institutional partner to open the right doors in the right markets."
— CGEC Founding Mandate

Our Principles

What Guides CGEC

01

Institutional Integrity

We operate with the standards expected of a government-adjacent body. Every recommendation, relationship, and programme is held to the highest standard of professional integrity.

02

SME-First Mandate

Our programmes are designed around the realities facing Canadian small and medium enterprises — the businesses that form the backbone of Canada's export economy.

03

Corridor Specialisation

We focus exclusively on UK, EEA, and India. Depth over breadth — knowing three markets extraordinarily well rather than twenty markets superficially.

04

Peer-Led Governance

CGEC is governed by Canadian business leaders who have themselves navigated international expansion. Practical wisdom, not theoretical advice.

05

Partnership Over Transaction

We build long-term relationships between Canadian businesses and their international counterparts — not one-off introductions, but enduring commercial partnerships.

06

Bilingual Accountability

CGEC operates fully in both official languages, honouring Canada's linguistic duality and ensuring accessibility for all Canadian businesses coast to coast.
