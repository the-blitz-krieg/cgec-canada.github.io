Our Organisation

Built to Open Global Doors

CGEC is Canada's dedicated not-for-profit council for international market expansion — combining Canadian expertise with 13 years of EICBI's institutional relationships across the UK, EEA, and India.

Our Mission

A Council Built on Institutional Credibility

The Canada Global Export Council (CGEC) was established to address a critical gap: Canadian businesses — particularly SMEs — possess world-class products and services, but lack the institutional relationships, regulatory knowledge, and on-the-ground networks required to succeed in complex international markets.

CGEC exists to close that gap. As the exclusive Canadian partner of the Europe India Centre for Business & Industry (EICBI), we bring 13 years of parliamentary-level relationships, EU institutional access, and proven trade corridor expertise directly to Canadian exporters.

We are a not-for-profit organisation. Our mandate is the success of Canadian business on the global stage — not shareholder returns.

"Canada's businesses are globally competitive. What they need is a credible institutional partner to open the right doors in the right markets."
— CGEC Founding Mandate

Our Principles

What Guides CGEC

01

Institutional Integrity

We operate with the standards expected of a government-adjacent body. Every recommendation, relationship, and programme is held to the highest standard of professional integrity.

02

SME-First Mandate

Our programmes are designed around the realities facing Canadian small and medium enterprises — the businesses that form the backbone of Canada's export economy.

03

Corridor Specialisation

We focus exclusively on UK, EEA, and India. Depth over breadth — knowing three markets extraordinarily well rather than twenty markets superficially.

04

Peer-Led Governance

CGEC is governed by Canadian business leaders who have themselves navigated international expansion. Practical wisdom, not theoretical advice.

05

Partnership Over Transaction

We build long-term relationships between Canadian businesses and their international counterparts — not one-off introductions, but enduring commercial partnerships.

06

Bilingual Accountability

CGEC operates fully in both official languages, honouring Canada's linguistic duality and ensuring accessibility for all Canadian businesses from coast to coast.

Governance

Transparent, Accountable, Not-for-Profit

CGEC is incorporated as a Canadian not-for-profit organisation, governed by a Board of Directors and subject to full regulatory compliance.

Legal Structure

CGEC is incorporated under the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act and operates with full transparency to its members and the public.

  • Not-for-profit corporation under federal law
  • Annual reporting to members and public
  • Independent financial audit
  • Board-approved annual budget
  • Bilingual governance documentation

EICBI Partnership Framework

CGEC operates as the exclusive Canadian partner of EICBI under a formal Strategic Partnership Agreement that defines the scope, responsibilities, and benefits of the relationship.

  • Exclusive Canadian representation of EICBI
  • Access to EICBI's parliamentary networks
  • Co-branding on joint trade missions
  • Intelligence sharing and market briefings
  • Joint programming and delegation support

Membership Accountability

CGEC member organisations have formal rights within the Council's governance structure, including representation at the Annual General Meeting.

  • Annual General Meeting for all members
  • Member feedback mechanisms
  • Programme performance reporting
  • Member advisory panels by sector

Conflict of Interest Policy

CGEC maintains rigorous conflict of interest policies to ensure that Board decisions serve the membership, not individual commercial interests.

  • Annual conflict of interest declarations
  • Recusal protocols for board votes
  • Independent ethics review
  • Whistleblower protection policy
