Membership Application

Begin Your Global Expansion
Journey with CGEC

Applications are accepted from Canadian businesses at all stages of export readiness. Our team reviews every application and responds within five business days.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

CGEC membership is open to Canadian-registered businesses with a genuine interest in expanding into the UK, EEA, or Indian markets. We welcome businesses at all stages — from those considering export for the first time to established companies entering new corridors.

We are sector-agnostic. CGEC works with businesses across technology, professional services, agri-food, clean energy, advanced manufacturing, financial services, life sciences, and more.

🍁

Canadian-Registered Business

Must be incorporated or registered to operate in Canada — federal or provincial registration accepted.

🌍

Export Intent

Must have genuine commercial interest in the UK, EEA, or Indian markets — at any stage of readiness.

✅

Good Standing

Business must be in good legal and financial standing, with no material regulatory or legal issues.

🤝

Commitment to the Council

Members are expected to engage actively with CGEC programmes and contribute to the Council community.

What Happens Next

  • 1

  • 3

  • 4

  • 5
    Welcome to CGEC — market entry support begins
